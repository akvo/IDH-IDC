# Product Brief: Income Driver Calculator (IDC)

## Problem Statement
Farmers in global supply chains often earn significantly less than the Living Income Benchmark. Companies and stakeholders lack precise, data-driven tools to identify which combination of income drivers—such as price, volume, land size, or cost of production—will most effectively close the income gap for specific farmer segments.

## Target Users
- **Sustainability Managers**: Aiming to design effective living income interventions.
- **Supply Chain Analysts**: Responsible for processing farmer data and identifying segments.
- **Procurement Officers**: Looking to understand the impact of price and volume changes on farmer livelihoods.

## Value Proposition
The IDC provides a high-fidelity simulation and modelling environment that translates complex farmer data into actionable insights. It allows users to segment populations, identify key income drivers, and model "what-if" scenarios to find the most feasible path to a living income.

## Core Features (MVP+)
1. **Interactive Segmentation**: Support for manual, categorical, and numerical segmentation with cascading boundary logic.
2. **Income Gap Visualization**: Deep-dive into household income composition vs. living income targets.
3. **Multi-Driver Analysis**: Heatmaps and 3-driver calculators to explore synergies between price, volume, and land.
4. **Advanced Scenario Modelling**: Graphical equation visualizer to simulate changes in primary, secondary, and tertiary commodities.
5. **Feasibility Gauges**: Real-time feedback on whether modelling targets are physically or economically reachable.

## Constraints & Assumptions
- **Data Quality**: Relies on accurate household survey data (CSV/Excel).
- **Benchmark Data**: Requires predefined Living Income Benchmarks for comparison.
- **Connectivity**: Primarily an online web application.

## Success Metrics
- **Modeling Accuracy**: Alignment between calculated gaps and benchmark targets.
- **User Insight**: Ability for users to identify at least one feasible "closing the gap" scenario per case.
- **System Performance**: Rapid recalculation of complex modelling scenarios across thousands of data points.
